Chair datapack

This minecraft datapack will add to the game many furniture, but for now beacause it's still in alpha it only add chairs and tables. You can craft this furniture and you will get spawn eggs to spawn them. To destroy them you need to do /trigger kill_chair_nearest. This furniture has functionality too on chairs you can sit and on tables you can put things. I plan to add many furnitures like sofa or tv beds and many more.
